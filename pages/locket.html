The internet is becoming more and more automated just like everything else. After all, why shouldn't it be? In search for a less painful website construction process, most turned to Wix and Webflow. I turned to my hands.

3+ years of web designing experience

This is me, and this is my website.

It deeply saddens me how everyone is so busy with everything. Superfluously. Scraping just the enough amount of getting the job done and the wage paid. The motives were never questioned, the emotives repressed and abandoned. We don't get paid for feeling things, and so we stopped feeling things. Simple logic. I'm not a counterpoint to all of this. But boy, do I wonder. I've met people who ring the bell over and over again for me: the less we speak, the more we communicate. I have my blessings for them, but they refuse to be vulnerable - even for a blessing. They argue eloquently yet faithlessly. They go through their days with no look on their face. I want their lives - no, I don't, I only yearn for their containment.
