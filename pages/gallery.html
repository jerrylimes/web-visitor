Art is about trying. Trying is inherently embarrassing.

On a late night on Friday, March 20, 2026, I couldn't believe I have the time to do what I've been postponing for 2 years. Building my gallery!

I was hesitant in gathering all my work and putting them here. Would that be nice? Yes. Would that be practical? No. Would that be aesthetically pleasing? Absolutely the fuck not.

So you can imagine how relieved I was when I found out I don't have to make a decision - so many of my early childish attempts at creating art have vanished. I might know where to find them, but it's best if I don't try to recall.
